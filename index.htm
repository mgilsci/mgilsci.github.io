Computational Science, Genomics, Language, Absurdism, Minimalism

I am a senior research fellow and lecturer in the Applied Computational Genomics Team, ACGT at the Institute of Applied Simulation, IAS at Zurich University of Applied Sciences, ZHAW.

My work involves various aspects (algorithmic, computational, statistical, modelling) of the pipeline from molecular sequences to phylogenies, with an emphasis on model/method assessment using real biological data, and on applications for biotechnology and biomedical research. In addition, I have a strong interest in computational semantics, transdisciplinarity and art.
